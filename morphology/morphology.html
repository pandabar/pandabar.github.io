English Morphology

This is an introductory course, therefore it doesn't delve too deep into theory. Here we just review the main processes of word formation, discuss a bit of typology, and the interfaces with phonology and syntax. Based largely on Lieber's Introducing Morphology, the contents are very accesible for first year students.
