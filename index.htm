Hola

I am a PhD student in Linguistics at the University of Manchester. My main areas of interest are Phonetics and Phonology, Second Language Acquisition and Speech perception. I have done some work in Artificial Intelligence and CALL, but now I am currently working on vowel perception in L2. My supervisors are Dr. Yuni Kim and Dr. Wendell Kimper.

If you wish to contact me, feel free to drop me a line at fernanda.barrientoscontreras(at)manchester(dot)ac(dot)uk.
