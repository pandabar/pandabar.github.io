Fernanda Barrientos

I obtained my PhD degree in Linguistics in 2018 from the University of Manchester, and I am currently an Academic Collaborator at the University of Konstanz. My main areas of interest are Spanish phonetics and phonology, Second Language Acquisition, speech perception and loan phonology. I did some work in AI and CALL/ICALL when I was a Master's student at the University of Concepción (Chile), but nowadays my focus in on L2 speech perception and production.
