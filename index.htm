Hola

I am an Academic Collaborator at the University of Konstanz. My main areas of interest are Phonetics and Phonology, Second Language Acquisition and Speech perception. I have done some work in Artificial Intelligence and CALL, but now I am currently working on vowel perception in L2. Newer projects include loanword phonology and (L1) vowel perception in Chilean Spanish.
